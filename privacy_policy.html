Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

Bangla Hub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Location information (city, state)

1.2 Location Data

We collect precise location data to:

  • Show relevant events and services in your area
  • Enable location-based search for tutors, services, and businesses
  • Allow you to post events with location information

You can disable location services in your device settings, but this may limit certain app features.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage data (features used, time spent, interactions)
  • IP address and network information

1.4 Content You Post

When you post events, job listings, business promotions, or service offerings, the information you provide becomes publicly visible.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Connect you with local events, businesses, and services
  • Send you important notifications about your account
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Analyze app usage to improve user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Public Information

Information you post (events, job listings, business profiles, service offerings) is visible to all app users.

3.2 Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party vendors who help us operate the app, including:

  • Firebase (Google) - for authentication and database
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Analytics services

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure Firebase authentication
  • Regular security updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Disable location services

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days, though some content may remain in backups or if shared publicly.

8. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification.

10. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our servers are located. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.
